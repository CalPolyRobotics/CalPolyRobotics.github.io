UROV

We are the Cal Poly Underwater Remotely Operated Vehicle (UROV/ROV) team. Coming from mixed majors and backgrounds, we are building a task-based vehicle for both competition and research in aquatic environments. The international MATE competition is held this summer in Houston, Texas from June 25-27, 2016. Students competing in this international collegiate level competition learn about submersible technology, and gain experience that guides them toward the technical marine workforce.

IGVC

The goal of the Autonomous Golf Cart Project is to retrofit a golf cart with all the sensors and motors to create an autonomous golf cart which can drive around campus. The golf cart is equipped with a LIDAR, stereoscopic camers, sonar rangefinders, GPS, hall effect sensors, and other sensors.
